## 中文馆 / Chinese Collection

### 经典大厂款
> 感谢您参与本次招聘流程。经过综合评估，我们认为您与岗位的匹配度暂未达到预期，此次暂不考虑。期待未来有机会合作。

**解读**：你没通过。原因不告诉你。我们不会联系你。

---

### 双向奔赴款（中小厂）
> 您好，感谢您对我司的关注与信任。经过慎重考量，很遗憾这次我们未能双向奔赴。您的背景令我们印象深刻，希望未来有缘再聚。祝您求职顺利！

**解读**：同上，但加了"双向奔赴"显得我们也很遗憾。

---

### 画饼安慰款
> 感谢您的投递！您的履历非常优秀，但结合现阶段团队需求，我们暂时无法推进。您的简历将保留在我们人才库，如有匹配岗位，我们会第一时间联系您。

**解读**：没有人才库。没有第一时间。

---

### 系统自动发送款
> 尊敬的候选人，您好。感谢您申请[岗位名称]一职。经过评估，您的申请未能进入下一轮。感谢您对[公司名称]的关注。

**解读**：这封邮件没有人类参与。

---

### 已读不回之后补发款
> 您好，非常抱歉未能及时回复您。我们已对您的简历进行了评估，遗憾地通知您……

**解读**：你简历投出去三个月了。

---

### 猎头消失前最后一条消息
> 您好！简历我已经转交给用人部门了，有消息我第一时间联系您！

**解读**：此后永久已读不回。

## English Wing

### The Corporate Standard
> Thank you for your interest in [Company] and for taking the time to apply for the [Role] position. After careful consideration, we have decided to move forward with other candidates whose qualifications more closely align with our current needs. We will keep your resume on file for future opportunities. We wish you all the best in your search.

**Translation**: You didn't get it. We won't tell you why. We will not contact you again.

---

### The Empathetic Startup
> Hi [Name], thank you so much for taking the time to meet with our team — we really enjoyed getting to know you. After much deliberation, we've decided to move forward with a candidate who is a stronger fit for where we are as a team right now. This was genuinely a difficult decision. We'd love to stay in touch and will absolutely keep you in mind for future roles. Wishing you all the best!

**Translation**: Culture fit. We will not stay in touch.

---

### The One That Took 3 Months
> Hi [Name], I sincerely apologize for the delay in getting back to you — this process took longer than anticipated. After an extended review, we've made the difficult decision to move in a different direction. We truly appreciate your patience throughout this process.

**Translation**: We forgot about you. Then we remembered. Then we rejected you.

---

### The Recruiter's Last Words
> Hey! Just wanted to follow up — I shared your profile with the team and they're very interested. I'll be in touch with next steps soon!

**Translation**: *(Last active 847 days ago)*

### The Almost-Offer
> We were incredibly impressed by your background and the conversations we had. This was one of the most competitive processes we've run, and the decision came down to very specific technical requirements for this particular role at this particular time. We hope you'll consider us again in the future.

**Translation**: We gave it to an internal candidate two weeks ago.

### The ATS Ghost

**Translation**: A robot read your resume for 6 seconds.
